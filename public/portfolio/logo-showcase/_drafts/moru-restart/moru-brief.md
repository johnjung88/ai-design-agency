## Status

This is a design-only draft package. It is not connected to the live portfolio.

## Direction

MORU Coffee is treated as a fictional premium small-batch coffee roastery. The new identity focuses on a compact, memorable emblem that can hold up across marketplace thumbnails, business cards, package labels, stickers, SNS icons, and signage.

## Core Idea

The symbol combines three ideas in one mark:

- `M`: the first letter of MORU and the strongest memory hook.
- Coffee bean: placed at the visual center to make the category immediate.
- Cup/saucer curve: adds warmth and makes the mark less generic.

## Visual Tone

- Warm espresso background
- Muted brass highlight
- Cream wordmark
- Editorial spacing
- Simple geometry that can be redrawn as a clean vector
